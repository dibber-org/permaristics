What is this again ?

This application targets permaculturists wanting to create a permaculture design by finding elements that have synergies in between them. One way to do so is by finding the needs and the products of each element. If what produces of an element is needed by another one, you have a synergy in between them.

With Permaristics, you'll be able to find new synergies or find elements that will add new ones to the design. You may start by either entering the needs or products you want to find synergies for, or by entering elements to which Permaristics will complete synergies.
